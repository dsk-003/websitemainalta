Disease Detection Using AI

The Artificial Intelligence team of Alta Futuris has ability to absorb information coming from a number of factors from symptom data, disease causes, test results, medical images, doctor reports and more. The team can help disease detection using AI and model how it will behave in a cell in combination with other drugs the patients might be taking. With machine learning and deep learning algorithm skills, the team can look at how we can identify the right patients and sites to run clinical trials. This would help to run shorter studies and show where the medication is the right one for those patients earlier.

With constant mutation of diseases and viruses makes it difficult to stay ahead of the curve, Alta Futuris team can help for diseases detection using AI so as to advance, creating new treatments and helping people live longer and healthier lives. The team is capable of developing solution that would automatically detect diseases characteristic features from a photo. Together with the clinical symptoms of the patients and genetic data, the team can make it possible to calculate with high accuracy which disease is most likely to be involved.
