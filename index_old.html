Artificial Intelligence


Machine learning and applications

Machine Learning enables the computers or the machines to make data-driven decisions rather than being explicitly programmed for carrying out a certain task. These programs or algorithms are designed in a way that they learn and improve over time when are exposed to new data. It is entering an array of enterprise applications and domains.

Deep learning

How do we get machines to learn more than just a specific task? What if we want it to be able to take what it has learned from analysing photographs and use that knowledge to analyse different data sets? This requires computer scientists to formulate general-purpose learning algorithms that help machines learn more than just one task.

Neural networks

Neural networks are a set of algorithms, modelled loosely after the human brain, that are designed to recognize patterns. They interpret sensory data through a kind of machine perception, labelling or clustering raw input. The patterns they recognize are numerical, contained in vectors, into which all real-world data, be it images, sound, text or time series, must be translated.

Blockchain/ Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT)


Immutability

Blockchain/Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) database is surprisingly super immutable. It’s one of the best features of this ledger system. In this distributed ledger, once you add anything to the ledger, you won’t be able to alter it, delete it or even reverse it.Immutability has the potential to transform the auditing process into a quick, efficient, and cost-effective procedure, and bring more trust and integrity to the data businesses use and share every day.

Enhanced Security

Hacking Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) is nearly impossible due to its decentralized nature and cryptographic encryption. The nodes make transactions using cryptography where every record on the network gets encrypted. Utilizing public and public key the transacting party ensures no one can intercept between a transaction.The use of a shared, distributed ledger offers a measure of resilience by limiting the impact of a cybersecurity incident experienced by any single node and preventing a single point of failure.

Consensus

Blockchain/ Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT) supports a wide range of consensus mechanism for validating a transaction. Consensus mechanisms are a way to reach an agreement between nodes on the network. When you are dealing with millions of nodes on the network, it gets extremely difficult to reach an agreement without the help of consensus. As this is a trustless environment, only the algorithms can help them maintain the distributed ledger. It’s one of the special features of Blockchain.
