Artificial Intelligence in Dentistry

Artificial Intelligence in Dentistry

Alta-Futuris team has shown great capabilities and capacities of Artificial intelligence in medical applications in recognition of meaningful data patterns. The team has used deep learning algorithm models as tools for clinical trials, especially, to aid in the decision‑making in each phase for diagnosis and subsequent treatment, as well as prognosis and projection.

AI-driven customized orthodontic treatment is getting widely popular in medical profession. AI is now available for orthodontic diagnosis, treatment planning, and treatment monitoring. With precise 3D scans and virtual models, it is easy to 3D print the aligners with customized treatment plan. As the vast data get computed, it creates an algorithm which in terms intelligently decides how a patient’s tooth or teeth should be moved, with how much pressure, even identifying pressure points for that particular tooth or teeth. The AI‑aided aligners not only deliver precise treatment execution but also helps in monitoring the progress as well and claim to reduce treatment time as well as appointment schedules.

Laboratories are using AI to automatically generate advanced dental restoration, designed to perfect fit and ideal function while exceeding aesthetic expectations. This not only will help dentistry but will have a huge potential and impact on orofacial or craniofacial prosthesis.

Alta-Futuris team consists of expert AI professionals having extensive experience in deep learning and they have done extensive solutioning for pre-treatment diagnosis and analysis of dental imaging with visual treatment recommendations that are accurately and instantly performed. Alta-Futuris AI team provides value to the dentistry stream; be it endodontics for apex location or implantology for making precise surgical guides and identifying type of bone to cortical thickness.
